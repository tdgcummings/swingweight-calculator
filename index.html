Golf Club Swing-weight Calculator

  1. Weigh the total club-weight as accurately as possible in grams.
  2. Find the clubs balance point and mark it on the shaft.
  3. Measure from the end of the grip cap to the balance point in inches.
  4. Enter the club name, weight and balance point into the fields below.
